Your Business
Basic context so we understand how your operation is structured. This information stays with the research — it is never used for sales outreach without your explicit permission.
Question 01
What does your business do and how does the work actually flow day to day?
Plain language is fine. What's the actual work, who does it, and how does it move from start to finish?
Question 02
How big is your operation?
Question 03
How would you describe your current use of technology in daily operations?
Question 04
Does your business currently use AI in any capacity — and if you've tried it before and it didn't stick, what happened?
No wrong answer. We want to understand where businesses in this area actually are with AI, not where they think they should be.
Question 05
As our research develops, patterns will emerge across similar businesses in your sector. Would you like us to reach out when findings relevant to your industry are available?
No obligation. This is research — not a sales follow-up. A yes just means we can share what we find.
Part 02 of 04
How Work Gets Done
We want to understand your actual operational reality — not the org chart version. How does information move through your business on a typical day?
Question 06
Walk us through a typical operational day — from the moment things start moving to the end of the day.
How does work get assigned, tracked, reported, and closed out? What tools or processes are involved at each step?
Question 07
Who hands work off to whom during a typical day — and where does something most often get lost, delayed, or fall through the cracks in that handoff?
Think about the moments when information moves from one person to another, or from the field to the office. Where does it break down?
Question 08
Who puts your reports together, how long does it take them, and how often does it happen?
Internal reports, client reports, compliance reports — whatever reporting your operation produces regularly.
Part 03 of 04
Where It Breaks Down
This is the section that matters most. Be direct — we're here to understand the operation, not judge it.
Question 09
What's the single most time-consuming thing your operation does that feels like it shouldn't take as long as it does — and how often does it happen and roughly how long does it take each time?
The thing that eats hours every week that you've always assumed was just part of the job.
Question 10
How long has that been the case?
Question 11
Where are you losing money that's hard to put a number on — and if you had to estimate what it costs the business monthly, what would you guess?
Missed follow-ups, jobs that slip through, billing errors, overtime caused by inefficiency — anything like that. A rough range is fine.
Question 12

Part 04 of 04
The Bigger Picture
Last few questions. These help us understand how operational leaders in this area actually think about their work and where the real friction lives.
Reminder: This is research. If anything you share surfaces a finding relevant to your industry, we'll reach out only if you indicated that's welcome — and only to share what we found, not to pitch anything.
Question 13
If you could see any single number about your operation updated in real time, what would it be?
Don't think about whether it's technically possible — just describe what you'd want to know at a glance.
Question 14
If you had a tool that could handle one operational headache automatically — what would it fix?
Don't worry about whether it's possible. Just describe the problem you'd most want gone.
Question 15
Is there anything about your operation, your industry, or the Permian Basin business environment that would be important context for understanding how you work?
Regulatory requirements, seasonal patterns, workforce dynamics, anything that shapes the way your operation has to run.
